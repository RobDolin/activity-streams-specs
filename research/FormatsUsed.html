Research into Current Feed Practice on Popular Sites and Platforms

Publishing Formats

The following table shows which feed formats are used on several different publishing platforms. Other than the base syndication format, the remaining columns are focused on the publication of media content such as videos, photos and audio.

This list is broken into sets based on the primary media published by each platform. The first section lists general weblog/microblog platforms. The second section lists photo/image publishing platforms. The third section lists video publishing platforms, the fourth section lists a single audio publishing platform while the fifth and final section lists activity aggregators whose feeds describe user actions rather than published content.

Some platforms actually support other formats but do not link to them on the page either by an in-page hyperlink nor by auto-discovery. Such feeds are not included in this table since unlinked feeds are not useful to most users.
